About Us!

Welcome to the official website of the James Madison University Men’s Club Volleyball team! We are a competitive, student-run organization that provides JMU students with the opportunity to play high-level volleyball, build lasting friendships, and represent JMU in regional and national competitions. Our team prides itself on fostering teamwork, dedication, and a love for the game. Whether you’re looking to compete or simply support our club, we’re excited to share our passion for volleyball with the JMU community!

Memories

"My fondest memory of being on the James Madison Men’s Club Volleyball team was the road trip to Nationals last year. Traditionally, the team flies to Nationals, but due to circumstances, we missed the opportunity and had to drive instead. Spending so much time together in vans really brought us closer as a team, and the long journey gave us the chance to make unforgettable stops in Nashville and Louisville. It was a great experience and a road trip I’ll never forget."
- Parker Davis
